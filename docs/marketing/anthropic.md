# marketing/anthropic.md — approaching Anthropic (the big guys)

How to earn an Anthropic feature/amplification + credits. Strategy: `docs/MARKETING.md` (§0 core
principle). Sequence: `docs/ROADMAP.md`. Siblings: `X.md`, `youtube.md`, `github.md`, `site.md`,
`kofi.md`.

## The one principle (internalize this)

**You do not market *to* Anthropic. You build something the community shares, and Anthropic
notices.** There is **no "submit to get featured" portal** — featuring happens *downstream* of a
project spreading among developers and sci-fi people (X, HN, AI newsletters, the Cookbook). Every
approach below is **artifact-first, ask-last**: you lead with the live station + the writeup, and the
"would you consider featuring this?" comes at the very end, once.

**The one concrete *direct* door is the Anthropic Cookbook** (a GitHub repo that openly accepts
community contributions). Everything else is earned.

**Why this project is amplifiable** — lean into it: it's **built entirely by Claude Code**, it's a
**multi-agent writers' room**, it's **honest about being AI** (spoken + on-screen disclosure), and
it's **safe by design** (the C0 gate). Anthropic amplifies things that are technically impressive
*and* values-aligned. Reliability and disclosure aren't compliance here — they're the reason you're
featurable. **A project that's risky or embarrassing will never be amplified**, which is why none of
this fires before the station is reliable (C9) and retaining people (M3).

**Timing:** the real outreach is **Phase D / M4**, *after* visible traction. Everything before M4 is
prep and being-a-good-ecosystem-citizen. Don't cold-pitch a half-built thing — you get one first
impression with devrel too.

**Audience:** Anthropic devrel + the Claude-ecosystem amplifiers (the people who surface cool Claude
builds). High-signal, allergic to hype, generous toward genuine contribution.

**Status legend (validated 2026-06-24):** `✅ READY` = actionable now; `📝 DRAFT` = gated on a later
milestone (M1/M4), validate then. **Only MA0 is live now** — and it's *ongoing groundwork*, not a
one-time post. Everything else waits, on purpose: cold-pitching before traction (M3) burns your one
first impression with devrel. The artifact does the work until then.

## The tasks

### MA0 — Be a good ecosystem citizen (now, quietly) · `✅ READY`
**Depends on:** nothing — **do now**; runs continuously.
**Goal:** by the time you approach, the groundwork already says "serious Claude build" — no
manufactured pitch needed.
**Do:**
- Make the **repo read as a serious Claude build**: the "built with Claude Code" badge (MG3), the
  honest agentic-build story up top, the safety gate + disclosure visible.
- On X, the **build-in-public threads** (MX3 safety gate, MX4 world clock, MX7 resilience) *are* the
  evidence — they show the engineering, not just the vibe.
- Engage **genuinely** where Anthropic devrel already are (reply with substance, answer questions,
  share what you learned). **Never** cold-DM "please feature me," never tag-spam. Give before asking.
**Done-when:** ongoing — the public footprint already demonstrates a credible Claude build.
**Result check:** organic signals — a devrel/ecosystem person engaging unprompted is the leading
indicator that M4 outreach will land.

### MA1 — Apply for Anthropic credits (the soft, early touch) · `📝 DRAFT`
**Depends on:** **M1** (a live public demo is enough proof) — part of the M2 funding track.
**Goal:** the lowest-stakes Anthropic contact — a credits application, not a feature ask.
**Do:** apply for **Anthropic self-serve / promo credits** with the live station as proof (alongside
the ElevenLabs + AWS applications — see `MARKETING.md §4`). Keep it factual: what it is, what it
uses Claude for, the live link.
**Done-when:** the application is submitted.
**Result check:** submitted ✓; approval = a bonus and a warm early data point, not a gate.

### MA2 — The case study (the artifact devrel actually shares) · `📝 DRAFT`
**Depends on:** **Phase D / pre-M4** (Claude drafts, you own and publish). *(Spine = the DEVLOG read
oldest-first; it's already accumulating that arc.)*
**Goal:** the **"I had Claude Code build a 600-year-future civilization"** writeup — the single asset
Anthropic-adjacent channels reshare.
**Do:** an honest, technical narrative — the multi-agent writers' room, the world clock, the safety
+ continuity gates, what was *hard*, what Claude Code did. Publish on the site/newsletter + GitHub.
Lead with the engineering and the honesty, not the hype.
**Done-when:** the case study is published and linkable.
**Result check (2wk):** reshares into dev/AI circles; it becomes the link you hand devrel in MA4.

### MA3 — The Cookbook PR (the one direct door) · `📝 DRAFT`
**Depends on:** **Phase D** (the writers' room is solid + generalizable) + **M4**.
**Goal:** contribute a **genuinely useful guide** to the **Anthropic Cookbook** — *a multi-agent
writers' room with the Agent SDK* — extracted from the real station. A contribution, **not** a
backdoor ad.
**Do:** distill the writers'-room pattern (showrunner → writers → editor/continuity → safety gate)
into a clean, runnable notebook/example others can reuse. Submit as a proper PR following the
repo's contribution guidelines. Reference the station lightly as the origin, not the subject.
**Done-when:** the PR is open and passes the repo's contribution bar.
**Result check:** PR engagement/merge; this is the most durable, highest-credibility touch — it puts
you *inside* Anthropic's own docs.

### MA4 — The devrel note (artifact-first, ask-last) · `📝 DRAFT`
**Depends on:** **M4** — only *after* visible traction (the launch spike + M3 retention holding).
**Goal:** a short, warm note that asks to be *considered* — once.
**Do:** a brief message to Anthropic devrel with: the live link, the case study, one line on why it's
interesting (built entirely by Claude Code, multi-agent, honest AI), and a soft ask. **Lead with the
artifact; the ask is the last sentence.** Send once; don't follow up more than once.
**Template:**
> Hi — I built Settlement Radio: always-on radio from the human future, broadcasting 600 years ahead
> as the one signal that holds a scattered humanity together across the dark between worlds. **Every
> word is written by Claude and the entire system was built by Claude Code** — a multi-agent writers'
> room, a world clock, and blocking safety + continuity gates, with on-air AI disclosure throughout.
> It's been live and unattended for [N weeks] and people are coming back.
>
> Live: [LINK] · How it was built: [CASE STUDY] · I also opened a Cookbook PR with the writers'-room
> pattern: [PR].
>
> If it'd be a fit for a Built-with-Claude feature, I'd love to be considered — happy to provide a
> case study in whatever form is useful.
**Done-when:** sent (once).
**Result check:** a reply; a feature is the win (the DM / commercial goal). No reply → the artifact
keeps working; don't chase.

### MA5 — The amplifier path (indirect, parallel to M4) · `📝 DRAFT`
**Depends on:** **M4** (the hero clip + case study exist).
**Goal:** reach Anthropic *through* the ecosystem — the AI newsletters and community amplifiers who
surface Claude builds.
**Do:** when the loud launch fires, make sure the **hero demo clip + case study** land where these
people look (HN, the right X circles). You don't pitch them — a great, honest demo is what gets
picked up, and Anthropic notices what its ecosystem is sharing.
**Done-when:** the launch assets are in front of the amplifier circles (part of the M4 push).
**Result check:** pickups/mentions in AI newsletters or by ecosystem accounts; each is an indirect
path to the feature.
