the tribute's truest home — a civilisation that tells itself wondering stories the way *we* love the
genre this whole station honours. What do the settled worlds revere, retell, and argue over?

**Building blocks to consider**

- The story-traditions the worlds revere — including the world's own "speculative" tradition.
- How art travels (slowly) between worlds, and what that does to a shared canon.
- The handful of works "everyone" knows.
- Artists as figures — leave room for D10 to give them names and quotes.

imaginative fiction. Reference traditions and themes only — **never** real authors, works, or
